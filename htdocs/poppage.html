Pop Music

Pop Music is a musical genre that is consists of popular songs, as stated and abbreviated in the name. The genre’s music usually aims to give entertainment to an urban universal audience, as it focuses mainly on mass appeal and commercial success instead of artistic creativity. What is considered to be “pop music” never stays the same for people’s preferences change over time, along with different breakthroughs and shifts in developing music. It is a super genre that can have any mixture of sound, so long as it is popular.

Almost every song in the pop genre has a simple structure that makes it easy to listen and sing along to. Said song structure often consists of verses, choruses and sometimes a bridge to build tension. The verses usually have different lyrics but are sung in the same way throughout the song. The chorus or hook is often the catchiest and distinctive part of a pop song. The bridge is the part of the song that breaks from the pattern and is used as a transition between different verses or as a build-up towards a final chorus. The songs in the genre usually last around two to four minutes. Pop songs have a basic melody and a danceable rhythm that makes it difficult for the song to leave a person’s head.

The appeal of pop music lies on its repetitiveness and simplicity. It is conservative for songs usually deal with simple subject matter like falling in love or living the life of the party. They are accessible everywhere, from TVs to the everyday radio. Its diversity in sound keeps it constantly changing and with its huge influence, it always remains a source of entertainment to many people all over the world.
